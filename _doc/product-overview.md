# Product Overview

## Product Name
*(Working title — to be confirmed during onboarding)*

## What It Is
A browser-based realistic soccer game featuring real-world players and teams. Users select from authentic squads, set formations, and play matches directly in the browser — no download, no installation, no hardware requirement.

## Users
Soccer and football gaming enthusiasts who want an authentic, instantly accessible match experience — fans who know the real players and want to play with them, without the barrier of consoles or heavy software.

## Core Promise
Play realistic soccer with real players and real teams, instantly, in any browser.

## MVP Scope
- Real player rosters with individual stats (speed, passing, shooting, stamina)
- Team selection from real-world clubs
- Playable match experience with authentic mechanics (fouls, formations, substitutions)
- Scoring and basic match events
- Post-match stats summary

## Tone & Brand
Energetic, authentic, sports-forward. Faithful to the spirit of the beautiful game — confident, not gimmicky.

## Strategic Principles
- **Realism first**: player behavior and stats mirror real-world performance
- **Zero friction**: instant play — no downloads, no accounts required for core gameplay
- **Depth over breadth**: one excellent match mode before expanding to leagues or tournaments
